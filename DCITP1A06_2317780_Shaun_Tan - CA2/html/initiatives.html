REDUCE OUR WASTE

Making waste of resources unacceptable must permeate every part of our lives. Every item we purchase has an impact on the environment and may wind up in a landfill. It is becoming more and more obvious how plastic waste affects our oceans and how severely it affects marine life. Recycling what we can reduces how many new items we are manufacturing, and upcycling is a clever approach to transform trash into something useful. For example, you could reuse an old tin as a planter or a jam jar as a candle holder..

WATCH WHAT WE BUY

We can all do more to exercise more awareness when choosing our purchases and sources. You'll spend less money, produce less waste, and have a smaller environmental impact if you buy less. Both you and the environment can profit from a less consumerist way of life. Make use of our purchasing power and make sure that our funds are funding constructive change. Supporting environmentally friendly products that cause less harm to the environment encourages businesses to source and create their goods sustainably.

STAY INFORMED

Keeping ourselves educated is one of the best things we can do; the more we know, the better. Our ability to have those talks with our friends, family, and the individuals we want to influence will improve as a result. Learn the facts, keep up with current events on the state of our natural world, and determine what you can do.

Living A Sustainable Lifestyle Using The 3Rs

Water

  • Turn off the tap when it is not in use, e.g. while brushing teeth, or washing hands
  • Avoid long showers; keep them to no more than 15 minutes

Electricity

  • Turn off lights and fans when they are not in use

Plastic

  • Choose items that have less packaging
  • Reduce the use of disposable cutlery by eating in
  • Invest in reusable cutlery sets

Sign Up For Activities Under The OneMillionTrees Movement

Reasons Why:

  • Joining these activities to plant trees allows us to create denser tree canopies
  • By having denser tree canopies, these canopies can reflect radiant heat and cool our surface
  • They can also help to mitigate the Urban Heat Island (UHI) effect and climate change

Host An Environmental Sustainability Event

Reasons Why:

  • To spread the message about environmental awareness and sustainability

Examples of How It Can Be Done:

  • Host a marathon in partnership with either the government/sport brands and provide step trackers
  • The marathon can make use of existing apps that track a participant's steps as well as carbon footprint to pair with the step trackers
  • This will allow participants to be more aware of environmental sustainability
